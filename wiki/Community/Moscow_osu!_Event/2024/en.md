# Moscow osu! Event 2024

![MOE 2024 logo](img/logo.png)

**Moscow osu! Event 2024** (***MOE 2024***) — the second iteration of the osu! festival, held from July 26 to 28, 2024, at **PhystechPark, Moscow, Russian Federation**, gathering over 450 people offline.
